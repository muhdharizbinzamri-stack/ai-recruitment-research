Investigating the Role of AI and Human Across Recruitment Stages

A Case Study in Malaysia exploring how Artificial Intelligence and human judgement collaborate throughout modern recruitment processes.

Explore Research View Findings

Research Overview

This study investigates the balance between AI automation and human involvement across six recruitment stages in Malaysian organizations.

AI Recruitment Systems

AI technologies improve recruitment efficiency through automation, resume screening, predictive analytics and candidate matching systems.

Human Decision Making

Human recruiters contribute emotional intelligence, ethical judgement, communication skills and contextual understanding during hiring.

Recruitment Challenges

Organizations face uncertainty regarding which recruitment stages should prioritize AI systems or human involvement.
