Research Methodology

Research Design

Quantitative cross-sectional research design will be used to collect data from HR professionals in Malaysia.

Target Respondents

HR practitioners, recruiters and hiring managers from organizations operating in Malaysia.

Sampling Method

Convenience sampling will be applied to gather responses efficiently.

Data Analysis

IBM SPSS and SmartPLS software will be used for statistical analysis.

Recruitment Stages Investigated

1. Job Analysis

Understanding job requirements and organizational needs.

2. Sourcing & Attraction

Attracting candidates through digital platforms and AI systems.

3. Screening & Shortlisting

Filtering candidates using AI algorithms and ATS systems.

4. Assessment

Evaluating candidate skills through tests and analytics.

5. Interview

Human interaction and communication assessment.

6. Onboarding

Integrating employees into organizational culture.
