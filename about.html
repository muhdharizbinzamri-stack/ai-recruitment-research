Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming recruitment by improving efficiency, automation and candidate processing.

However, human recruiters remain important because recruitment also requires emotional intelligence, communication and ethical judgement.

Problem Statement

  • Organizations are unsure where AI should be used in recruitment.
  • Overusing AI may reduce fairness and candidate trust.
  • Underusing AI may reduce efficiency and increase workload.

Research Objectives

  • Investigate AI involvement across recruitment stages.
  • Investigate human involvement across recruitment stages.
  • Identify suitable recruitment stages for AI and humans.
