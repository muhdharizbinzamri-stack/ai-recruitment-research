Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has transformed recruitment practices by enabling organizations to automate repetitive hiring tasks, improve efficiency and reduce recruitment costs.

AI technologies such as Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), resume screening tools, predictive analytics and chatbots are increasingly used in recruitment processes.

Despite these advancements, human recruiters remain essential because recruitment requires empathy, ethical judgement, communication and interpersonal interaction.

Problem Statement

Overdependence on AI

Excessive reliance on AI may reduce fairness, transparency and candidate trust.

Lack of Human Interaction

Candidates may experience reduced emotional connection during recruitment.

Unclear Recruitment Roles

Organizations remain uncertain about which recruitment stages should prioritize AI or humans.

Research Objectives

  • Investigate AI involvement across recruitment stages.
  • Investigate human involvement across recruitment stages.
  • Identify suitable recruitment stages for AI systems.
  • Identify recruitment stages requiring human judgement.
  • Develop understanding of hybrid recruitment practices in Malaysia.
